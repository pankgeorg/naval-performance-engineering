## About the Program

ΠΜΣ-ΑΔΕΠ (Postgraduate Program in Ship Performance Analysis and Management) is a graduate program
at the **National Technical University of Athens (NTUA)**, School of Naval Architecture and Marine Engineering.

The 2026/2027 academic year covers seven core topic areas:

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Ship Resistance & Propulsion" icon="rocket">
    Hydrodynamic resistance, propeller theory, powering calculations, and
    sea-trial analysis.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Ship Manoeuvring" icon="random">
    Equations of motion, manoeuvring criteria, simulation, and full-scale
    trials.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Energy Efficiency" icon="sun">
    EEDI/EEXI/CII regulations, energy audits, hull & propeller optimization, and
    waste-heat recovery.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Condition Monitoring" icon="magnifier">
    Hull fouling, propeller degradation, performance indicators, and voyage data
    analysis.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Weather Routing" icon="information">
    Metocean data, seakeeping in waves, route optimization, and speed–power
    relationships.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Fleet Management & Analytics" icon="bars">
    Noon reports, KPIs, data pipelines, and fleet benchmarking methodologies.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Regulations & Decarbonization" icon="approve-check">
    IMO strategy, alternative fuels, Carbon Intensity Indicator compliance, and
    FuelEU Maritime.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
